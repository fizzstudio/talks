AI Descriptions

You give us data, we generate in parallel:

  • An accessible chart
  • A live description of the chart

We use a small AI engine, running in the browser, to discover and describe trends and patterns.

This is the modern equivalent of alt text and long descriptions for a high-leverl overview of a data visualization.

Quick, equivalent access

Sighted users usually glance at a chart, get the gist, and quickly move on.

Screen reader users should have the same affordance. No more, "Did I miss something important?"

We have 2 goals:

  1. Provide full interactivity and exploration of charts
  2. Help users avoid using our charts
